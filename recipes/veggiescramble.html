Veggie Scramble

Description

The current Veggie Scramble is considered a Hall of Fame Breakfast and was invented by Regina Marie Feola in 2024, in Philadelphia, PA. Regina experimented morning after morning perfecting the dish, both in flavor and nutritional value. Those who have tasted the dish can attest to it's amazing taste, but also great utility as it can be prepared in a large batch for meal preparation purposes.

Ingredients

  • 1 White onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • 10 oz. Spinach
  • 2 cartons of eggs
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Nutritional Yeast
  • Topped with Reduced Fat Cheddar Cheese
  • Hanks Sauce
  • Salsa

Cooking Instructions

  1. Dice 1 onion
  2. Sautee with Garlic
  3. Add spinach, cook til wilted
  4. Add egg mixture
  5. Add seasonings
  6. When eggs are almost done cooking add tomato
  7. Top with cheddar cheese
  8. Drizzle with Salsa
