Steamed Clam Recipe

Description

Steamed clams is a favorite dish amongst sea food lovers looking for a quick and delicious snack or meal. The recipe below will give your tongue an orgasm, which is light and playful.

Ingredients

  • 2 Dozen Clams
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Steamer
  • Saucepan
  • Water

Cooking Instructions

  1. Place the steamer inside the saucepan
  2. Add a small layer of water to cover the bottom of the pan
  3. Hint: Don't add too much water, you don't want the water to come above the strainer or touch the clams
  4. Add Clams
  5. Turn stove on high to boil water and begin steaming
  6. Cook 20 minutes
  7. While the clams cook melt the butter and garlic in microwave or on stove, whichever is preferred
  8. After 20 minutes or when clams begin to open take the clams off the stove
  9. Enjoy with the garlic butter concoction
